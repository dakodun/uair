RenderBatch
Overview
Processes and renders a batch of Renderables using a VBO. When a Renderable is added to the batch (via the Add function) its vertex and index data is computed (via a call to its Upload function) and added to the store. Then – when the batch's Upload function is called – it is sorted by texture id, split into segments (of matching texture ids) and uploaded to a VBO; the store is then cleared.
